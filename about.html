About the Creator

Kathy

Hi, I'm Kathy and I am the web developer and creator of Common Ground. Living in New York City has inspired to create this web friendly site in order to help the homeless, and to provide resources for anyone who is searching for it. By creating this website, I do not intend to solve the homelessness problem in my community, but to provide easily accessible resources for those that want to help and for those that need help. I hope I can further build upon this website to create something that would have a bigger impact on my community. My interest in computer science pushed me to use technology to engage myself in my community, and I hope I can continue doing so in the future.
