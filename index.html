This website is dedicated to having resources available to all people relating to a wide range of needs. If you or someone you know is homeless and you would like to easily access resources regarding the programs available to them, click on the “Do you need help?” hyperlink located on the Common Ground home page. Common Ground has also been created to bring awareness to this issue and to encourage more people to volunteer and help the homeless, if you are interested in learning more click on the “Do you want to help?” hyperlink also located on the homepage.
